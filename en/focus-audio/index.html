The science behind it

NeuroSinc is based on a phenomenon known as brainwave entrainment — sometimes referred to as binaural beats.

This is the tendency of the brain to align with external rhythmic stimuli. By delivering slightly different audio frequencies to each ear, the brain perceives a third frequency and begins to follow it.

Our sessions were designed by an expert and lab-tested over 2+ years to identify the frequencies that best support states of focus, calm, and sleep.
