Frequently asked questions

Why do I need NeuroSinc headphones instead of an app? +
Standard earbuds and meditation apps fail because regular speakers cannot accurately produce the deep, isolated frequencies required for binaural beats to work. NeuroSinc headphones are engineered specifically to deliver these precise neuro-acoustic frequencies.
What is NeuroSinc? +
NeuroSinc is a complete hardware and acoustic audio system that uses specific sound frequencies to help guide your brain into states of focus, calm, or deep rest.
How long should I listen? +
We recommend sessions of 15-30 minutes, depending on the state you want to achieve. Most users notice the effects within the first 10 minutes.
Are there any side effects? +
Our audio sessions are completely natural and use standard sound frequencies. However, if you have a history of seizures or epilepsy, you should consult a doctor before listening to binaural beats.
