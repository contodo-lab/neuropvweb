The science behind it

NeuroSinc is based on brainwave entrainment — sometimes referred to as binaural beats.

This is the brain's natural tendency to align with rhythmic sound patterns. By delivering slightly different frequencies to each ear, the brain perceives a third tone and begins to follow it — shifting its rhythm from overactive to calm.

Our calm sessions were developed by an expert over 2+ years of lab testing, specifically designed to support states of mental stillness and reduced overactivity.
