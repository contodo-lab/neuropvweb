The science behind it

NeuroSinc is based on brainwave entrainment — sometimes known as binaural beats.

This refers to the brain's natural tendency to align with rhythmic sound patterns. By delivering slightly different frequencies to each ear, the brain perceives a combined signal and gradually slows its rhythm toward rest.

Our sessions were designed by an expert and lab-tested over 2+ years to identify the exact frequencies that best support calm and sleep preparation.
